# Dashboard Blueprint

## Executive Overview

- KPI band: Headcount, Attrition Rate, High Risk Employees, Average Tenure, Estimated Replacement Cost, External Pressure Score
- Trend and distribution: Attrition Trend, Risk Band Distribution
- Prioritization: Retention Priority by Department
- Composition: Attrition by Department
- Insight block: short text summary

## Attrition Risk

- KPI band: High Risk Employees, Average Risk Score, Top Risk Driver, Overtime Rate
- Core views: Risk by Job Role, Risk by Salary Band, Main Risk Drivers, Recommended Actions

## Labor Market Context

- KPI band: Unemployment Rate, Wage Index, Labor Demand Index, External Pressure Score
- Core views: External Pressure by Occupation Group, Departments Exposed to External Pressure, Labor Market Trend

## Layout Notes

- Use `Executive Overview` as the public landing page.
- Keep the supporting dashboards simpler and more explanatory than the main page.
- Reuse filters across dashboards where possible for consistent navigation.
